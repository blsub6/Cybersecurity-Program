# Third Party Risk Policy - Basic

## Purpose
To ensure third-party vendors protect organizational data.

## Scope
All vendors, contractors, and partners with access to organizational systems or data.

## Policy Statement
Third parties must meet minimum security standards.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Procurement/IT Team:** Assess security of third parties.  
- **Managers:** Monitor vendor performance and compliance.  
- **Users:** Follow approved third-party procedures.

## Policy Requirements
- Use only approved vendors.  
- Include security requirements in contracts.  
- Monitor third-party security practices periodically.

## Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in contract termination or business risk.

## Exceptions
Must be approved by management.

## Review and Revision
Reviewed annually.
